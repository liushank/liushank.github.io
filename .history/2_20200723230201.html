Don’t be shy!
I know I won’t!

Hey there! I am a Dutch 3D illustrator with a consistently playful, fun and cheeky style. I started working and experimenting in 3D after my degree in Graphic Design and I’m hooked ever since. I worked and collaborated with brands such as Apple, New York Times and Pull & Bear and worked for both digital and physical projects. With a use of bold colours, characters with charm and a graphic CGI style, I try to create artwork that sparks joy and brings huge smiles on people’s faces!
